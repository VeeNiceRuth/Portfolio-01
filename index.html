I have been working in a non-profit organization for the past two years in City Heights

We provide a health clinic for people with no health care, free from charge aas well as fresh produce three days a week.

It has been such a joy to see organizations provide their services to people with little to no income.

I want to be able to help these organizations provide people easy applications to use to access their services i.e. free food, clothing, medical, and all at free from charge

I enjoy seeing the progress City Heights in particular has made and the love for that community has only grown

City Heights is known for its large population of immigrants from Taiwan, Sudan, Vietnam, Mexico, El Salvador and many more.
